## What does pandas do that normal Python lists cannot?

Pandas allows me to perform math on thousands of rows of data instantly without writing complex for loops, and it automatically handles messy CSV files by organizing them into a spreadsheet-like structure called a DataFrame.

## What is groupby() and what problem does it solve?

The groupby() function solves the problem of categorizing and aggregating data by acting like an instant pivot table. Instead of writing complicated dictionary logic, it lets me group all sales from a specific package or customer and calculate their total revenue with just one line of code.
